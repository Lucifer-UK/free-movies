1. Sarpatta Parambarai 2021




Watch Now

Sarpatta Parambarai is a 2021 Tamil-language sports drama film directed by Pa. Ranjith. It stars Arya in the lead role alongside an ensemble cast including Pasupathy, Dushara Vijayan, John Kokken, and Kalaiyarasan. The film is set in the 1970s and revolves around the fierce rivalry between two boxing clans in North Madras — Sarpatta Parambarai and Idiyappa Parambarai.

The story showcases Kabilan (played by Arya), a passionate but troubled man, as he rises from a humble background to become a boxer while facing personal and societal challenges. The film is known for its gritty portrayal of boxing, social issues, and themes of identity, respect, and redemption.

2. Mahaan 2022




Watch Now

Mahaan is a 2022 Tamil-language action thriller film directed by Karthik Subbaraj. The movie stars Vikram and his son Dhruv Vikram in lead roles, marking their first on-screen collaboration. Simran and Bobby Simha also play significant roles.

The plot follows Gandhi Mahaan (Vikram), a man who deviates from his family's strict Gandhian principles and becomes a liquor baron. His life takes a turbulent turn when he faces his estranged son Dada (Dhruv Vikram), who opposes everything Mahaan stands for. The film delves into themes of freedom, loyalty, family dynamics, and redemption.

3. Jai Bhim 2021




Watch Now

Jai Bhim is a powerful 2021 Tamil-language legal drama film directed by T.J. Gnanavel and produced by Suriya, who also stars in the film alongside Lijomol Jose and Manikandan. The story is inspired by a real-life case fought by Justice K. Chandru in 1993.

The film highlights issues of caste discrimination, police brutality, and systemic oppression. Suriya plays Chandru, a determined advocate who fights for justice for Senggeni (Lijomol Jose) and her husband Rajakannu (Manikandan), an oppressed tribal couple falsely accused and brutally mistreated by the police.

4. Vendhu Thanindhathu Kaadu 2022




Watch Now

Vendhu Thanindhathu Kaadu (2022) is a Tamil-language action-drama film directed by Gautham Vasudev Menon and written by B. Jeyamohan. It stars Silambarasan TR (Simbu) in the lead role alongside Siddhi Idnani and Radikaa Sarathkumar. The film features music composed by A. R. Rahman.

The story follows Muthu (Simbu), a young man from a humble background who gets entangled in the dangerous underworld of Mumbai. As he navigates violence and betrayal, he transforms into a powerful figure in the criminal world, grappling with moral dilemmas and survival challenges.

5. Dharala Prabhu 2020




Watch Now

Dharala Prabhu (2020) is a Tamil-language romantic comedy-drama film directed by Krishna Marimuthu. It stars Harish Kalyan, Tanya Hope, and Vivek in key roles. The movie is an official remake of the Hindi film Vicky Donor and deals with the topic of sperm donation, which is rarely explored in Tamil cinema.

The story follows Prabhu (Harish Kalyan), a carefree young man who is persuaded by Dr. Kannadasan (Vivek), a fertility specialist, to become a sperm donor. What follows is a mix of comedy, emotional moments, and romantic drama as Prabhu navigates his unique situation while falling in love with Nidhi (Tanya Hope).

6. Kadaram Kondan 2019




Watch Now

Kadaram Kondan (2019) is an action thriller directed by Rajesh M. Selva and produced by Kamal Haasan. The film stars Vikram in a stylish and intense role, alongside Akshara Haasan and Abi Hassan. It features music by Ghibran.

The plot follows KK (Vikram), a mysterious and highly skilled agent, who becomes entangled in a deadly conspiracy in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia. Parallelly, a young doctor, Vasu (Abi Hassan), and his wife Aathira (Akshara Haasan) are caught in a dangerous situation where KK becomes their only hope for survival.

The movie is packed with high-octane action sequences, sleek visuals, and twists that keep the suspense alive. Vikram's rugged look and powerful performance were highlights.

7. Viruman 2022




Watch Now

Viruman (2022) is a Tamil-language rural action drama film directed by M. Muthaiah and produced by Suriya under 2D Entertainment. The film stars Karthi and Aditi Shankar in lead roles, with Prakash Raj playing a pivotal character. This marked Aditi Shankar’s debut in Tamil cinema.

The story revolves around Viruman (Karthi), a righteous and spirited village youth who opposes his authoritarian father Muniyandi (Prakash Raj), whose selfish actions have torn their family apart. The film portrays themes of family bonds, revenge, and redemption, with plenty of emotional moments, traditional values, and vibrant rural settings.

The movie was praised for its strong performances, colorful portrayal of village life, and folk-style music composed by Yuvan Shankar Raja.

8. Kaappaan 2019




Watch Now

Kaappaan (2019) is a Tamil-language action thriller directed by K.V. Anand and produced by Lyca Productions. The film stars Suriya, Mohanlal, Arya, and Sayyeshaa in prominent roles, with music composed by Harris Jayaraj.

The story follows Kathir (Suriya), an elite Special Protection Group (SPG) officer assigned to protect the Indian Prime Minister (played by Mohanlal). As political conspiracies, terror threats, and betrayals unfold, Kathir finds himself caught in a deadly mission to save the nation while dealing with complex relationships and personal dilemmas.

9. Rocketry: The Nambi Effect 2022




Watch Now

Rocketry: The Nambi Effect (2022) is a biographical drama written, directed by, and starring R. Madhavan in the lead role. The film marks Madhavan's directorial debut and is based on the life of Nambi Narayanan, a former ISRO scientist and aerospace engineer who was falsely accused of espionage.

The movie traces Nambi's early contributions to India's space research, his advanced research work abroad, and the wrongful arrest that tarnished his reputation. It highlights his relentless pursuit of justice and his undying dedication to the country's scientific advancement.

10. Naane Varuvean 2022




Watch Now

Naane Varuvean (2022) is a Tamil psychological thriller directed by Selvaraghavan, starring Dhanush in a dual role alongside Indhuja Ravichandran, Elli AvrRam, and Selvaraghavan himself in a supporting role. The music for the film was composed by Yuvan Shankar Raja.

The plot revolves around twin brothers Prabhu and Kathir (both played by Dhanush) with contrasting personalities. While Prabhu is a loving family man, Kathir is a psychopathic killer. Their paths cross in unexpected ways when Prabhu's daughter starts exhibiting strange behavior, drawing him into a dark and chilling mystery involving his twin.
